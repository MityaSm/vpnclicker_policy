# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our idle clicker game ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

**Local Game Data:**
- Game progress (money, business levels, timers)
- Upgrade purchases and unlock status
- Ads watched counter and boost timers
- Offline reward calculations
- Player preferences and settings

This data is stored locally on your device using Unity PlayerPrefs and is not transmitted to external servers.

### Third-Party Services

Our Game may use third-party services that collect information to provide functionality:

**Advertising Services** (when implemented):
- Ad viewing frequency
- Device identifiers for ad delivery
- Ad interaction data
- Device type and OS version

**Analytics Services** (if implemented):
- Session duration and frequency
- Feature usage statistics
- Crash reports and performance data
- General device information (model, OS version)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Save and restore your game progress
- Provide offline earnings calculations
- Deliver rewarded video advertisements
- Optimize game performance
- Fix bugs and improve user experience
- Ensure fair gameplay

## Data Storage and Security

**Local Storage:**
- All gameplay data is stored locally on your device
- Data persists until you uninstall the game or manually reset progress
- We do not have access to your local game data

**Security Measures:**
- Data is stored using Unity's standard PlayerPrefs system
- No personal identification information is collected or stored
- No account creation or login required

## Third-Party Advertising

When ad functionality is implemented, the Game may display advertisements from third-party ad networks. These networks may:
- Collect device identifiers and IP addresses
- Track ad impressions and interactions
- Use cookies and similar technologies
- Serve personalized advertisements based on your interests

Common ad networks we may use include:
- Unity Ads
- Google AdMob

Please review the privacy policies of these services:
- Unity Ads: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy
- Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The Game is designed as general entertainment and does not require personal information to play.

## Data Retention

- Game progress data is retained locally until you uninstall the app
- Ad-related data is managed by third-party advertising services according to their policies
- You can manually reset all game data using the in-game reset function

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Delete Your Data:** Uninstall the game or use the "Reset Progress" function
- **Opt Out of Personalized Ads:** Use your device settings to limit ad tracking
  - iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking
  - Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **Access Your Data:** All game data is stored locally and accessible through gameplay

## Changes to Game Data

The Game includes a "Reset Progress" function that allows you to:
- Delete all saved game progress
- Clear purchased upgrades
- Reset ad viewing counters
- Return to initial game state

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

## International Users

The Game is available worldwide. By playing the Game, you consent to the transfer and processing of data as described in this Privacy Policy, regardless of your location.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

## Third-Party Links

The Game does not contain links to external websites or services, except for advertisement content served by third-party ad networks.
