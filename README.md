# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 4, 2026**

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how "VPN: Idle Tycoon" ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding our data practices in compliance with Google Play Policies.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

**Local Game Data:**
- Game progress (currency, business levels, timers)
- Upgrade purchases and unlock status
- Ads watched counter and boost timers
- Offline reward calculations
- Player preferences and settings

This data is stored locally on your device using Unity PlayerPrefs and is not transmitted to external servers.

**Device and Other Identifiers:**
- We collect device-specific identifiers, including **Advertising ID (AAID), Android ID, and IP address**.
- This data is collected automatically through third-party SDKs (e.g., Google AdMob) for advertising and analytical purposes.

### Third-Party Services
The Game integrates third-party services that collect information to provide functionality:

**Advertising Services (Google AdMob):**
- Ad viewing frequency and interaction data.
- Device identifiers for ad delivery.
- Device type and OS version.

**Analytics Services:**
- Session duration and frequency.
- Feature usage statistics and performance data (crash reports).
- Device identifiers to distinguish unique users for game optimization.

## How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:
- Save and restore your game progress.
- Provide offline earnings calculations.
- Deliver advertisements (including rewarded videos).
- Optimize game performance and fix bugs.
- Comply with Google Play Developer Policies.

## Data Storage and Security
- **Local Storage:** All gameplay data is stored locally on your device.
- **Security Measures:** Data is stored using Unity's standard PlayerPrefs system. Data transmitted by third-party SDKs is encrypted in transit using HTTPS.
- **No Personal Identification:** We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.

## Children's and Family Privacy
The Game is designed for a general audience, including users aged **9 and older**. We comply with the Google Play Families Policy:
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
- We restrict data collection to persistent identifiers necessary for internal operations.
- Advertisements are filtered to ensure they are age-appropriate.

## Data Retention
- Local game data is retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data in device settings.
- Ad-related and analytical data is managed by third-party services according to their policies:
  - [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  - [Unity Privacy Policy](https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy)

## Your Rights and Choices
- **Delete Your Data:** You can delete all local data by uninstalling the Game or using the "Clear Data" option in your Android device settings.
- **Opt Out of Ads Personalization:** Use your device settings to limit ad tracking:
  - Android: *Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization*
- **Access Your Data:** All game data is stored locally and accessible through gameplay.
